Graphic Design

Flyers, Banners, and Logos Created for Weber State University Student Association

Overview

During my time at Weber State University, I worked closely with clients to help make their visions a reality. As a graphic designer for the Weber State University Student Association (WSUSA), I created a wide variety of flyers, TV displays, logos, stickers, and social media campaigns. Creating content for students on campus was a wonderful experience as it allowed me to consider my audience in ways I hadn't thought before, such as editing captions and information to make them short and easy to read, finding the most marketable spot on campus with the most foot-traffic, and how to design content that can be printed on merchandise outside of digital or printed content. Overall it was a very beneficial experience and a great way to gain real-world experience with graphic design.

The majority of graphics were created using Adobe Illustrator, which is my preferred program of choice. I also used a lot of Adobe Photoshop and a bit of Adobe XD.

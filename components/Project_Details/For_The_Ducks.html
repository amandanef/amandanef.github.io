For The Ducks Prototype

User Experience, Wireframing, Adobe XD

VIEW PROTOTYPE

Background

One of the most fulfilling parts of working in web development and user experience is the fact that you get to work with real-life companies to create real-life solutions that really make a difference. For this project, my team and I were asked to design and create a new mobile application for a company or cause of our choice. We chose the "For the Ducks" foundation, which educates the community about the effects of dumping pet ducks, how to take care of ducks, and what the community can do to help rescue local ducks.

Research Methods

For this project, our team needed to find the best way to educate the community about ducks by finding the best way to organize information and draw the user's attention. We conducted a variety of user tests, along with design sprints and a handful of prototypes. By studying from users, we learned about what users are looking for in a mobile app: for example, social media contact buttons, "Secure Donation" icons when donating money, confirmation popups, and back arrows. Each user test and user feedback, no matter how big or small, aided in the creation of this prototype!

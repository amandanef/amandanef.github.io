Cartoons for Cancer

A fundraiser for Primary Children's Hospital

The Project

When I was diagnosed with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia, I discovered my love for drawing cartoons. That discovery lead to a life full of art, drawings, crafts, and hope, especially during such a hard time. To give back, I decided to use my talent and begin a fundraiser where I could raise money by drawing for people. That money would then be used to purchase art supplies for children in the oncology department at Primary Children’s hospital in Salt Lake City, Utah. Together we raised $225 worth of art supplies! I speak from experience that the gift of art goes a long way.

This fundraiser took place during November-December 2020. The logo was inspired by my own experience as a cancer patient in 2009, compared to myself as a young adult 11 years later.

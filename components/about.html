About Me

My Story

My name is Amanda Nef! I graduated Weber State University in Utah in Fall 2022 with a Bachelor's Degree in Web Design and User Experience, with Certificates in User Experience Design and Web Essentials. I pride myself in academics that reflect a 3.89 GPA and a technical degree which requires attention to detail. While my interests have always lied in computer science, my real passion lies with animation, story development, music, and art.

My story starts at a young age when I was diagnosed with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia. During those 3 years of chemotherapy, I found that cartoons, music, video games, and drawing were a source of hope and healing for me. To this day, I greatly look up to the animators, composers, voice actors, and content creators that dedicate their lives to making others smile. One fateful day in particular, I was able to meet my heroes from my favorite television series, Phineas and Ferb, thanks to the Make-A-Wish foundation! Since then, I've been inspired to become an animator in hopes that I can make people smile, just like they did for me.
